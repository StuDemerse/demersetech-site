Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your information

Last updated: April 18, 2026

DemerseTech ("we," "us," or "our") is operated by Stuart DeMerse, based in Midlothian, Texas. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and what rights you have regarding your data.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information in two ways:

  • Information you provide directly — When you fill out our contact form, you provide your name, email address, business type, and a description of your needs. We use this information solely to respond to your inquiry.
  • Information you enter into our free tools — Our AI-powered tools (Workflow Audit, Bio Generator, Email Follow-Up Generator, Meeting Notes Summarizer, Invoice Follow-Up Generator) process the text you enter to generate a response. This content is sent to our backend server and processed through the Anthropic Claude API.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to consultation requests and inquiries submitted through our contact form
  • To generate AI-powered outputs in response to your tool inputs
  • To improve and maintain our website and tools
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate our website and tools:

  • Anthropic — Our AI tools are powered by Anthropic's Claude API. Text you enter into our tools is processed by Anthropic's servers. See Anthropic's Privacy Policy for details.
  • Formspree — Our contact form is processed by Formspree. See Formspree's Privacy Policy for details.
  • Stripe — Payment processing is handled by Stripe. We do not store your payment information. See Stripe's Privacy Policy for details.
  • GitHub Pages — Our website is hosted on GitHub Pages. See GitHub's Privacy Statement for details.
  • Cloudflare — We use Cloudflare for DNS and email routing. See Cloudflare's Privacy Policy for details.

4. Cookies and Tracking

Our website does not use tracking cookies or analytics tools. We do not track your behavior across websites or build advertising profiles. Basic technical cookies may be set by our hosting provider (GitHub Pages) or Cloudflare for security and performance purposes.

5. Data Retention

Contact form submissions are retained by Formspree and forwarded to our business email. We retain email correspondence for as long as necessary to fulfill your inquiry or maintain our business relationship. Tool inputs are processed in real time and are not stored on our servers beyond what is required to generate your response.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of any personal information we hold about you. To make such a request, contact us at contact@demersetech.com. We will respond within 30 days.

7. Children's Privacy

Our services are intended for business owners and adults. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under the age of 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of our website and tools after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

  • Email: contact@demersetech.com
  • Location: Midlothian, Texas
